School Achivements

Change Maker School Award

S.B.O.A Public (Sr.Sec) School, South Chittoor receives the Green School Programme’s “ CHANGE MAKER AWARD “, which is instituted by the Centre for Science and Environment New Delhi. The school receives the award for making a measurable improvement in the green initiatives taken by the School. The school conducted audits on various parameters like land, air, water, food , waste and energy and took steps to improve the management of these resources . The GSP inspection team found a remarkable change in the sustainable utilization of these resources. The change in the biodiversity over the years, the rain pits to recharge the water table, the water harvesting system, the conversion of waste to fertilizers, recycling of paper and plastic, nil e –waste generation are some of the green school practices appreciated by the team. In recognition of these, the school won the GSP CHANGE MAKER AWARD 2018-19. The GSP coordinator of the school Mrs. Reny Daniel and two students Miss Emmy M Panicker and Vaneesha Benny received the award from Ms. Sunita Narain, Director General , CSE on 6th Feb at the award function held in New Delhi. CSE felicitated 12 Greenest schools from across the country. Over 1600 schools participated in the GSP from all over the country.

Places at the school

The Classrooms

Smart Class

Smartclass is a digital initiative, which is rapidly transforming the way teachers teach and students learn in schools with innovative and meaningful use of technology. Powered by the world’s largest repository of digital content mapped to Indian School Curriculum, smartclass brings in technology right next to the blackboard for teachers in the classrooms. Students learn difficult and abstract curriculum concepts watching highly engaging visuals and animations. This makes learning an enjoyable experience for students while improving their overall academic performance in school. The school has tied up with Educomp for setting up Smartclass in the school classrooms. With the introduction of EDUCOMP SMART CLASS teaching has been made more interesting for the students.

Laboratories

Well Equipped Laboratories

Laboratory techniques are the sum of procedures used on natural sciences such as chemistry, biology, physics in order to conduct an experiment, all of them follow scientific method; while some of them involves the use of complex laboratory equipment from laboratory glassware to electrical devices others require such specific or expensive supplies.
